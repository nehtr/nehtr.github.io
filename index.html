Nim

Good knowledge of Nim, a powerful compiled language with the performance of C, but way faster to write. Due to its high compatibility with Python, C/C++, and JavaScript, it suits well to accelerate (up to 40 times) bottleneck processes in Python. Since it can generate native dependency-free executables, it's also an optimal choice to deploy end-user software.
